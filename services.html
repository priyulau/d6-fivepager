Services

Academic Tutoring

My specialties include all levels of chemistry, biology, physics and other sciences. I also can support math up through Pre-Calculus and other academic subjects at the high school level. My approach begins with understanding the class structure, student's strengths and weaknesses, and parent/student goals for our work together. I typically see students twice a week for one hour in order to maintain a regular cadence where we can review prior class materials and assignments, work on current homework/classwork, strengthen content understanding, and, if time allows, preview upcoming material for better processing during classtime.
